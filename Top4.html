Top 4. USS Oriskany - Pensacola, Florida

Location: Approximately 26.5 miles Southeast of Pensacola, Florida
Basic Information: The aircraft carrier was originally constructed as part of the war effort, but after World War II ended, the construction slowed down and did not start until 1947. After going though few redesigns, the USS Oriskany earned two battle stars during the Korean war and five stars during the Vietnam War. On may 17,2006, the world's largest intentionally-created artificial reef was sunk near Pensacola, Florida. Since then, the wreck has become a habitat for diverse species of marine lives. Diving at this site is challenging due to strong currents and depth.

Marine Life: small tropical fish like blennies, damselfish and angelfish to large game fish like snapper, grouper and amberjack.
difficulty: advanced or Deep Diver Specialty Certification needed
